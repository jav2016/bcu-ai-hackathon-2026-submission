What The Challenge Wants

  • Answer 100 multiple-choice questions from questions_100.csv.
  • Submit exactly question_no,answer in TEAMNAME_submission.csv.
  • Answers should be A, B, C, D, or E.
  • Include code, README, and a short PPTX/PDF in your own GitHub repository.
  • Any LLM used must be 8B parameters or smaller.

Pipeline I Am Building

  • 1Clean the question text and repair encoding artifacts.
  • 2Build targeted search queries from the question and entity names.
  • 3Retrieve evidence from DuckDuckGo and Wikipedia.
  • 4Search each answer option separately to avoid weak guessing.
  • 5Score options using exact phrases, keyword overlap, numbers, and co-occurrence.
  • 6Export answers plus an evidence log for auditability.

Why This Method

These questions are mostly Wikipedia/general-knowledge facts. A pure LLM may hallucinate. Evidence-first retrieval is more practical, easier to explain, and safer under the 8B model rule.
